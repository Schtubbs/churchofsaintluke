The Sacraments

Christian initiation is accomplished by three sacraments together: Baptism, Confirmation, and the Eucharist. These sacred gifts are the foundation of our faith and the source of God's grace in our lives.

Faith Formation

Growing in faith is a lifelong journey. Our comprehensive faith formation programs serve children, teenagers, and adults, guiding them deeper into their relationship with Christ through scripture, prayer, and community.
