Our Ministries

Serving the Greater Stroudsburg Area in Word and Sacrament

Our mission is to bring Christ to a diverse community, while inviting and drawing all into the unity of Christ. Through our many ministries, parishioners find meaningful ways to live out their faith and serve others.

Eucharistic Ministers

Bringing the Body of Christ to the faithful at Mass and to the homebound.

Altar Servers

Young parishioners serving faithfully at the altar during liturgy.

Lectors & Commentators

Proclaiming the Word of God at weekend and weekday Masses.

Bulletin Stuffers

Volunteers who prepare and assemble our weekly parish bulletin.

Knights of Columbus

A brotherhood of Catholic men serving parish and community.

Columbiettes

Women's auxiliary of the Knights of Columbus.

Ministry Fair

Each year we host a Ministry Fair to help parishioners discover how to get involved. Come find your place in our parish family and explore the many ways you can share your gifts with our community.
